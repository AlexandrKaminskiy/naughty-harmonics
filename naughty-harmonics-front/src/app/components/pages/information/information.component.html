About Naugthy Harmonics
"Naughty Harmonics" is a unique application designed for guitar enthusiasts, offering an innovative way to create and share guitar tabs. Unlike traditional methods, which often involve manually transcribing notes and chords, Naughty Harmonics utilizes advanced audio processing algorithms to analyze recordings and automatically generate accurate guitar tabs.
